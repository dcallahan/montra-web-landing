# Page Structure & Content Specification

## Target Audience Profile

**Primary:** Women 25-45 managing family finances
**Psychographics:**
- Busy professionals balancing career and family
- Overwhelmed by financial complexity
- Want control without becoming finance experts
- Value time-saving tools
- Appreciate approachable, non-judgmental guidance

**Pain Points:**
- "I don't know where our money goes"
- "Budgeting apps are too complicated"
- "I'm afraid I'm not doing this right"
- "I don't have time to track everything"

**Desires:**
- Confidence in financial decisions
- Peace of mind about family's future
- Simple answers to money questions
- Partner in financial wellness journey
